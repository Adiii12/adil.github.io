About Me

Fascinated since a young age by all things metaphysical, my life turned upside down in 2015 when I found myself on the path of Shamanism, and which revealed to me all that I had been seeking. Continuously deepening my journey and studies, I have since then opened my own healing practice, and have become a Tuina therapist - the hands-on branch of Traditional Chinese Medicine. I am also a Kambo practitioner, Kambo being a poisonous frog - yes you read that right - that has its roots in the ancient world of Amazonian Shamanism, and which has incredible health benefits that continue to be studied by Western science.
